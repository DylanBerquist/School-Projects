Background:

My name is Dylan Berquist. I am originally from Pinckney, Michigan. I graduated from Pinckney Community Highschool in 2017. I graduated with a small group of students participating in the "New Tech" alternative to normal high school. New Tech is a completely separate set of classes oriented around project-based learning. I found the majority of my best friends through this program, considering the small size of the program, we were basically forced to get to know and work with one another.

I only have one sibling, my older sister Tina. I have a pretty big family, considering all of the separating and rebuilding my entire family has gone through. All of the major holidays are total chaos for my sister and I. There are usually around 3-4 celebrations for each of the major holidays. Which means a lot of gifts I have to buy for everyone, which also means draining my entire paycheck every time.
